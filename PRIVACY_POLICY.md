# Privacy Policy for Read It Later

**Last Updated**: November 16, 2025

## Introduction

Read It Later ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

The Extension stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- **Page URLs**: The web addresses of pages you save to your reading list
- **Page Titles**: The titles of the web pages you save
- **Timestamps**: The date and time when each page was saved

This data is stored using Chrome's local storage API and **remains on your device**. We do not have access to this information.

### Data We DO NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

- ❌ No personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
- ❌ No browsing history beyond the pages you explicitly save
- ❌ No passwords or credentials
- ❌ No payment information
- ❌ No tracking or analytics data
- ❌ No cookies
- ❌ No device information
- ❌ No location data

## How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally is used solely to:

- Display your saved reading list in the side panel
- Allow you to access your saved pages
- Prevent duplicate entries in your reading list

**We do not transmit, sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose.**

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only**: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your reading list is not synchronized to any cloud service or external server
- **No External Transmission**: The Extension does not send any data to external servers, third parties, or our servers
- **Your Control**: You have complete control over your data and can delete items at any time

## Third-Party Services

The Extension **does not**:

- Use third-party analytics services
- Integrate with external APIs
- Share data with third-party services
- Include third-party tracking scripts

## Permissions Explanation

The Extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | To save your reading list locally on your device |
| `activeTab` | To get the title and URL of the current page when you click "Add Current Page" |
| `tabs` | To open saved pages in new tabs when you click on them |
| `sidePanel` | To display the reading list in Chrome's side panel |
| `host_permissions` (https://\*/\*) | To access page metadata (title and URL) from any secure webpage |

**Important**: These permissions are only used for the functionality described. We do not use them to collect, transmit, or misuse your data.

## Data Retention

- Data is retained **indefinitely on your device** until you manually delete it
- You can remove individual items from your reading list at any time
- Uninstalling the Extension will automatically delete all stored data

## Your Rights and Controls

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access
You can view all your saved pages in the Extension's side panel at any time.

### Deletion
- Delete individual items by clicking the trash icon next to any saved page
- Delete all data by uninstalling the Extension

### Export
Your data is stored in Chrome's local storage. While the Extension doesn't provide a built-in export feature, your data can be accessed through Chrome's developer tools if needed.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The Extension is intended for general audiences and does not target children.
